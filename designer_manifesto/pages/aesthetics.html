Usage

  • The ultimate goal of the designer should be for the user to find utility in their user interfaces
  • Minimalism in user interface design can restrict the tools that super users and professionals can use in efficiently traversing their workflows

Identity

  • The visual identity of a design should be distinct and easily recognizable to the user.
  • Making a functional design visually interesting helps ensure users do not get burnt out or overencumbered by the functionality of the interface.
