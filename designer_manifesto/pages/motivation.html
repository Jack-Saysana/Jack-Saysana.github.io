Beauty

  • There is great fulfillment to be found in the ambition to create something beautiful/elegant, whether it be technical or aesthetic
  • Beautiful works of art are inspiring for both the producer and consumer, which increases the likelihood of further great works

Creation

  • Creation is the greatest form of individual expression
  • Creation is the ability to define a legacy, which may work to outlast its creator

Engineering

  • High level problem solving in pursuit of creating a well made solution grants fulfillment to an engineers life
  • Solution oriented creation facilitates the fusion of technical and aesthetic skills in an solve problems
