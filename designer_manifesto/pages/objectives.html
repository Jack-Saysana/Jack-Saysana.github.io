Innovation

  • The more one creates, the more likely it is they push themselves to innovate and improve their own work
  • Through seeking distinction in one's work, they may find themselves pushing not only the bounds of their own skill, but also the bounds of their medium and domain

Problem Solving

  • Design has the primary goal of providing some sort of value to the world
  • In user interface design, this primarily takes the form of solving users' problems
  • In the pursuit of problem solving, creativity is used to achieve both artistic and technical value
