Void Scavenger is a rogue-like dogfighting and first-person-shooter inspired by many popular games such as Star Wars ™️ Squadrons and Doom ™️. The game takes the player through intense firefights and fun, yet challenging, flights through the stars on their path to upgrade their ship and fend off enemies and aliens for as long as possible.

Features:

> Space Combat

  • Pilot a starship and take to the skies!
  • Face challenging enemies of varying ship types

> FPS Gameplay

  • Traverse a randomly generated space station
  • Fight against alien enemies in doom-like combat
  • Level up to gain new skills and abilities

> Ship Customization

  • An in-depth ship customization system
  • Scavenge new and novel parts as you traverse stations
  • Upgrade and improve the capabilities of your ship

> Rouge-like Gameplay

  • Each space-mode level is randomly generated with unique enemies
  • Space station layouts are procedurally generated and differ run-to-run
  • Strive for a higher and higher level each playthough

> Open Source

  • Source code fully availible for modification and download on GitHub

Technologies Used:

  • Written fully in C
  • Utilizes my OpenGL engine for common graphical and physics workflows
